Eclectic Eats

Eclectic Eats offers food from around the world in a casual setting. East African doro wat and injera is a specialty as is vegetarian Indian cuisine including favorites like chana masala and dal. Mexican tamales with red and green chili sauce and homemade tortilla chips tempt diners.
